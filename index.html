Vacation Time?

Come and stay at Villa CortezView, the best two-bedroom, two-bath (six person maximum) Luxury condo at the Las Mananitas Phase 1.

Features

Air Conditioned. Washer/Dryer. Jetted Tub in Master Bath. DVD, IPod Dock, Stereo. 65" TV with Digital Content. Local Minnesota Channels. Full Kitchen: Dishwasher. Gas BBQ Grill Poolside. Phone, Internet. Direct Access to Beach. Master Suite with King. 2nd Bedroom with 2 Queens. 2 Baths Sleeps 6.

Activities

Onsite Gym and Tennis. Nearby Golf, Parasailing, Surfing, Horseback Riding, ATV's, Fishing, Close to Puerto Los Cabos, Hiking, Shopping, Jet Skiing

Rating

Las Mananitas was rated #1 Speciality Lodging in all of Los Cabos by TripAdvisor for several years and in the top 5 most years. Las Mananitas is at the West end of the Mega Resorts Blvd (Paseo Malecon). Walking distance to Supermarket, Shops, Dining.

Activities

Did we mention activities? Besides shopping, road trips, food, there are tons of things to to for a complete vacation. For the golfers, there is a vast choice of golf Courses around Las Mananitas. courses designed by the likes of Jack Nicklaus, Tom Weiskopf, Robert Trent Jones II, Pete Dye, Greg Norman are all there. For non-golfers there is fishing, ATV's, dinner cruises, snorkeling, horseback riding, live music and offroading just to name a few.

Features

Villa CortezView (Unit D306) is a 2020-square-foot, luxurious condo on the top floor, (third) and the geometric center of the complex. Only the third-floor units in this complex have vaulted ceilings; so you get a feeling of vastness in this one. It has a commanding view of the beach and the pool area. Lavishly decorated, Villa CortezView has a full Kitchen (Range, Fridge, Microwave), Digital TV Service in Main Living Room as well as Master Bedroom, Air conditioning, and all amenities expected in a five-star condominium.
